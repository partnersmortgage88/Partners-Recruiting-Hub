Founder & Servant Leader

Dan Trinidad

CEO & Founder · Partners Mortgage

Dan founded Partners in 1988 with one unwavering belief: everyone matters. Customers, referral partners, the community, and above all, the people on his team. That philosophy hasn't shifted in 37 years.

He entered the industry as a loan officer, became a top producer at a Savings and Loan in Saratoga, then left to start Partners. Under his leadership the company has funded over 60,000 loans and become one of the most respected independent mortgage companies in the country.

Partners For A Cure

Motivated by the loss of his wife to cancer, Dan founded Partners For A Cure in 2011. What began as 20 teammates has grown into over $1 million raised for the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society.
